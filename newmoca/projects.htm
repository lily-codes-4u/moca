Projects

This category is largely reserved for art that may be considered Post-Modern or maybe Post-Post-Modern. The art may not be conventional. The art may be in the concept; it may be in the presentation; it may even be in the mind of the artist, made manifest. It is the project itself that is the art. Indeed, the parts composing the project may not have aesthetic interest at all. It is their inclusion in the project that gives the authority of art to them. The project is the mother of the art.
