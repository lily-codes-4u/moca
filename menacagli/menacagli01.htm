Stefano Menicagli
Time Machine
(1 of 12)
Stefano Menicagli has extraordinary Photoshop skills. His creative journey began with photography and with explorations of different kinds of figurative and abstract images, before he discovered digital image manipulation. He also does mathematical fractals (not shown here). The 12 images included in this exhibit were all created in Photoshop. He lives and works in Tuscany, Italy.
