Steve Bingham

enhanced photography

Steve Bingham is 65 years old. He has been taking photographs since childhood, has a Master of Science Degree in Photography from the Brooks Institute of Photography in Santa Barbara, CA, has taught photography on the high school and college level and conducted his own commercial photography business for years. He lives with his wife in the small town of Payson, Arizona. His work has been published in many national magazines and exhibited in many Arizona galleries. He was one of the first photographers ever to go digital, publishing an article in 1994 in Popular Photography entitled "Digital Photography Comes of Age." Almost nothing has escaped his vision. From nudes to nature, his genius is pictorial, sometimes sentimental and unconflicted, but always forthright and clear-eyed. He is a Photoshop master.
