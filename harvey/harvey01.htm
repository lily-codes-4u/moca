Fantasies of Spam Email
Adam Harvey
Sensational Revolutions in Medicine
(1 of 7)

Adam Harvey writes, "The theme of the work is repurposing ambient internet data and exploring alternative digital imaging techniques. For this series, entitled SAVE AS: Spam, Porn and Art, I've created a custom algorithm that pulls email subject lines directly from my inbox and maps them into erotic forms that play out the fantasies of the spam emails."

Adam Harvey is a web designer and photographer living in Brooklyn, NY. His works focuses on exploring alternative digital imaging techniques and contemporary social phenomenon. He studied Integrative Arts at Penn State University and will be pursuing his Masters in the Interactive Telecommunication Program at New York University in the Fall of 2008.
