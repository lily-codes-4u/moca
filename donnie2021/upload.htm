How to send your images

Send images via Gmail (or other email program) by email attachment to:

don@moca.virtual.museum

or:

donarchr@gmail.com

Six image maximum. Still images in JPG format. Images should be about 1 to 2 MBs in transmission size.

Images are posted online chronologically as received, usually within 24 - 36 hours of receipt. You will be advised when your art is online.

NOTE
Payment is a separate step and may be made before or after uploading. Click PayPal or credit card link top of page to make payment.

Thank you and good luck in the contest.
