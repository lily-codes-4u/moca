Romesh Mistry

My venture in to cyber art (or digital art) started in 1996. Playing with the computer, I found the tools and effects available within it amazing. It gave unlimited scope to soar high in to the world of imagination with very high speed.

Awards: won 2nd prize in an international competition ?DIGITALISM 1? organized by MOCA (Museum of Computer Art), Brooklyn, NY, USA, in October 2008; won 10th place in the international competition ?Top Ten Digital Realities? organised by Artrom Gallery, Rome, Italy in January 2009; selected in an international exhibition on the theme ?Celebrate Her Story? organized by Manhattan Arts International, NY, USA in 2009.

One Man Shows: Amdavad ni Gufa, Ahmedabad; Ravishankar Rawal Art Gallery, Ahmedabad; Shrishti Art Gallery, Vadodara; ANZ Grindlays Bank Art Gallery, Mumbai; Nehru Centre, Mumbai; Contemporary Art Gallery, Ahmedabad
