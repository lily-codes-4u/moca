Orna Ben-Shoshan

channelled art

Orna Ben-Shoshan was born on a kibbutz in Israel in 1956 and studied graphic design in Tel-Aviv. She lived for 15 years in the United States, returning to Israel in 1996. Her work explores an alternative reality, a parallel world. It is spiritually driven. So is Orna. She tells us that she "receives the image through channelling." Her work is completed in her mind before it is drawn. It is replete with detail and humor. It has been widely exhibited in the United States and Israel.
