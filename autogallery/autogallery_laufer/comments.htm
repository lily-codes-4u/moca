Tammy Mike Laufer

"Tammy Mike Laufer is a Designer and an Artist. Live and work in ISRAEL. Her professional education from the Israel 'Technion' Graphic Design Degree. And Continuing education program for Designers at Sivan College Tel Aviv. Throughout the last decade where she worked as Art Director design and developed Computer games and 200 Hebrew fonts, she also dealt with Computer Art. She creates 3D digital art ,Video art, contemporary art and short films. In 2009 she participated at the Biennale in Venice. Member of IMPACt, the Professional Visual Artists Association of Israel."
