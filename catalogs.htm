Catalogs of our Exhibits and Contests

MOCA publishes a series of catalogs of our shows and contests in collaboration with Blurb.com, the online publisher. You can order up to 31 different catalogs. All are careful and elegant publications, printed on gloss paper in authentic full-color. Each image entered into the event is reproduced on its own page.

For the student of digital art, this series records the history of digital fine art in our era. For the participating artist, these catalogs serve as a valuable promotional tool and self-advertisement.

Series designer is Steve Soper. Editor is MOCA director Don Archer. Prices range from $55.00 to $119.00 depending on format, size and number of pages. Shipping is extra.

Click any catalog cover to preview or order it at Blurb, the publisher.
