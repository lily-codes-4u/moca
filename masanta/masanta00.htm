Masanta


an art of high spirits

NOTES
Masanta is a Japanese artist of high spirits. She studied graphic design at Japan Designers College in Tokyo, then continued her studies at the Setsu Mode Seminar for 18 months. Her art is warm, humorous and sunny, despite such subjects as poison, traps, prey and tears. She has has exhibited widely and won many awards for her art. She uses Photoshop, Illustrator, Macromedia Fontographer, pen, acrylic colors, mixed media. Two of her ink drawings conclude this exhibit, more evidence of the wit and charm of her drawn line. Her cat Lan is always on her lap as she works.
