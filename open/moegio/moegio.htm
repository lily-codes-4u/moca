Mo e Gio Pagni

The artists write, "Mo e Gio Pagni is the fruit of the artistic and creative union of Monica Citro (diploma from the School of the Polytechnic of Design in Milan and graphic designer and art director since 1983) and Giorgio Pagni (diploma from the Academy of the Brera in Milan and graphic designer since 1980). United as a couple not just in life but also in work, they set up their own studio of graphic design and communication in 1993 after meeting in 1990. Since 2005 they have developed an in-depth knowledge of graphic, drawing and photography programs which has led them to experiment and elaborate their own artistic compositions, paying particular attention to tones, movement and light. Using the techniques and technologies which they have perfected across the years means they can communicate and express emotions in a new way. They have both discovered a new passion, a passion which as always they share together."
