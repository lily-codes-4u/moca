Grizelda Petrovska

"Grizelda is a technology coordinator and computer science teacher at Nova International Schools in Skopje, Macedonia. She has created a number of interactive non-commercial on-line Flash games like Who Wants to be a Millionaire?, Tic-Tac-Toe, Minesweeper, and Memory Game. For years she's been engaged in desktop publishing, designing books, brochures, posters, and ads. Fractal art has grown to be her true passion and challenge by the end of 2007. The images featured here are designed using fractal software only, without any post-processing."
