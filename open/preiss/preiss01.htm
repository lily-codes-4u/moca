"An award-winning illustrator, photographer and graphic designer, Alex uses digital photo collage to express his original thinking and unique vision. His work has been featured in publications as diverse as Clean Run, Hana Hou (Hawaiian Airlines in-flight magazine), Honolulu Magazine, and HMSA's Island Scene. His company, Kennedy & Preiss Graphic Design, is well known for their problem-solving abilities, specializing in the design and production of brochures, booklets, annual reports, logos, advertising, and other print materials. Clients include educational, health care, arts and humanities organizations, utilities, corporate communications, and real estate development and management companies. Kennedy & Preiss has earned over 90 local, regional, national and international professional awards. Alex has exhibited art in many media, including drawing, painting, sculpture, photography and digital photo collage. Since 2000, he has had four one-man shows of his photo collages in Hawaii at the Canon Photo Gallery, The Academy Art Center at Linekona, Gallery on the Pali, and the Hawaii Pacific University Gallery. Recently he was featured in group shows at The Contemporary Café at The Contemporary Museum, and Details Gallery. In 2006, Alex was one of the 241 distinguished artists from 49 countries featured in EXPOSÉ 4, a limited edition book published by Ballistic Publishing, France, showcasing 317 digital artworks selected from over 5,000 submissions."
