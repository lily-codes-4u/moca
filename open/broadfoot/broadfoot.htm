Thomas Broadfoot

Received an Honorable Mention at the at The Next Picture Show Gallery in Dixon Illinois. January, 2008

Received an Honorable Mention in the MOCA (Museum of Computer Art) October 2006 monthly contest. My personal website was selected in 2004 as a MOCANET distinguished computer art site.

Selected as the Digital Color Artist of the Month for April 2007 at Digital Consciousness.

Thomas is an emerging artist who designs digital abstracts, surreal scapes, and fantasy works based on the thoughts that run through his mind. His digital geometric designs are filled with perspective and vivid colors as well as being most characterized by the juxtaposition of opposing elements, color, value, and direction. Most of the digital abstract, surreal and fantasy artworks designed by Thomas over the years were more of his doodling when working on ideas, projects or applications. A defining moment occurred when he picked up a digital camera for the first time and began working with it. Since then, he has found that he enjoys the process of creation and that he is skilled at creating images and ideas with this technology.
