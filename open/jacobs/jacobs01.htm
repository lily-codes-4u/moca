"The art of Tracy Raven Jacobs was produced in the mid '90s. Tracy's pictures are entirely 3D modeled. Vivid 2.0 & P.O.V. ray tracers were used to produce the images. No scanned images were used. The jpeg images here have been compressed and sized down to make them suitable for transferring over the internet. The original image files are very large, up to 400 megabytes."
