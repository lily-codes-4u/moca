The artist states, My artwork is created to support self actualization and positive transformation. By working with universal symbols, my goal is to connect to (and recognize) the universal consciousness of the audience. As human beings becomes more aware of positive energies of the universe, we can translate those energies into positive actions in our day-to-day lives...art can be a positive force for good.

This first submission to MOCA is intended to encourage people to vote.

Bob T, is a digital fine art painter and mixed media artist. His artistic experimentation has lasted for the past 25 years and includes the development of innovative photomontages and other mixed media techniques. Much of his work combines traditional printmaking, painting and photography, combining digital and mixed media using pigmented and dye-based inks.
