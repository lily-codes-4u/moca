Sara G. Umemoto

"I'm a Mexican digital artist who started drawing in my childhood in my homeland and progressed in painting as a teenager during five years at the Academy of Fine Art. In 1995 I obtained a Diploma in Graphic Design. Since 1996 I have been living in Japan. In 2000 I started playing around with Adobe Photoshop and Corel Draw, and in 2001 I began to create my own style. Actually I do digital photography, digital paintings, and photo manipulations or photo-illustrations. All are creations processed using Adobe Photoshop."
