Christopher John Hobby


"I have been creating digital art for a little over two years. I taught myself, and developed my own techniques. I do not use Photoshop, and I create original unique templates to create my designs. Every design incorporates several of the same templates that I manipulate into any shape and design I visualise. Basically, at the heart of all my digital work are natural seashells. When I began creating my digital work, I first digitally photographed my previous collection of 3d multimedia sculptural work at macro scale, then selected elements from the images to create my digital template."
