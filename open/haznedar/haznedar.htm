Banu Haznedar

The 24 images in this series are from a series of multimedia artworks by Banu Haznedor called Circle.

Banu Haznedar is a Turkish artist and designer. She graduated from the Fine Arts Academy, Istanbul, in 1981. She writes, "I worked as a jewelery designer at a well-known jewelery company in Istanbul, then opened my own gallery on Bagdat Avenue, Istanbul, in 1990, and exhibited only my own works. Moving my gallery to Macka, Istanbul, under the name BIS Art Gallery, I exhibited my works, as well as that of other artists, from 1995 to 2003.

"Since 2003 I am concentrated on oil painting and computer art."
