WHO WE ARE
This site is the primary site of the Museum of Computer Art. It hosts the digital fine art
of thousands of digital artists and thousands of their images. Founded 30 years
ago, it has now established a complementary cross-linked site hosting
the spectacularly new and vibrant art of Artificial Intelligence.
Clicking links on this page accesses all art on both sites.

THIS IS WHAT WE DO
There's no charge to viewers. No charge to artists. No advertising. No Facebook or Meta. No Twitter. No distractions.
Dedicated solely to the online exhibit of digital fine art in its various forms and manifestations.
