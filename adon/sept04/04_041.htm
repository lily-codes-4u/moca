Many of the images in this series rely on geometric Islamic art, often overlayed or collaged with fractals or scraps of fractals, although some have no fractal component at all. Some images are almost wholly derived from the painted classics of such artists as Paul Klee and are also without fractal themes or motifs. Other images are pure fractals that may have religious (six-pointed star) significance. In all cases, I have substantially relied upon a large collection of textures in an effort to give depth and character to the art. The intent is always to be dramatic and innovative.
