WE WANT YOUR ART. YOUR GALLERY HERE IS FREE

MOCA welcomes submission of your art, including (AI) Artificial Intelligence Art. Your submission and gallery are free. Upload no less than five and no more than 12 images. Image size 1 - 2 MBs. There are no membership or registration requirements, and never any charge. We welcome almost any style of art or photography so long as the artist employs some level of digital input or technique. All art is permanently posted to our AutoGallery 2024.

(AI) Artificial Intelligence Art especially welcome, drawn in any AI program.

ALL UPLOADS OF IMAGES ARE BY EMAIL TRANSMISSION
Send images to
don@moca.virtual.museum

In the body of the email please feel free to add a biographical note and/or curriculum vitae and make a brief comment on your art and technique. If sending (AI) Artificial Intelligence art, feel free to advise the program in which the images were drawn, and you may even supply the prompts that drew the art.

IMPORTANT NOTE ON IMAGE TITLES AND SIZES
Image titles should be brief and succinct. Image titles are used to title your images on the Autogallery page (with the JPG extension stripped out). This is software controlled, so titles should be carefully edited by you to reflect the exact titles you want.

Upload size 1 - 2 MBs per image. 5-12 images only.

JPG format. Thank you!
