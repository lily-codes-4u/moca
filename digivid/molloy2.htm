Light Weaving
by Brenda Molloy

This is an animated fractal. Playing time 2:57 minutes.

Bbrought to life by the music of Mike Hovancsek.

Brenda Molloy lives with her husband in upstate New York, near Buffalo. She is a fractal enthusiast. She says that this animation "is meant to be a place to rest the mind in these very interesting times. A friend of mine has told me that it lowers her blood pressure."
