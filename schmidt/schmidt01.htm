Marcos Schmidt is an illustrator and graphic designer in São José dos Campos, Brazil. He says he is a self-educated artist, yet does not despise what he has learned in architectural and art courses he has taken. He calls these works "Illustrations." They usually start as conventionally-drawn watercolors, oils, acrylics, graphite, etc. and are then digitally re-worked in Photoshop and Corel Painter. He is blessedly married to Giovanna, which he says makes him the happiest man alive.
