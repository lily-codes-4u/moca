Thomas Broadfoot is known by the alias BySilent. Indeed, he tells us almost nothing about himself or the art he creates. He is prolific, and says matter-of-factly, "I am a self-taught digital artist that enjoys the creation of work that speaks to the viewer." It is clear that he is a careful student of 3d rendering and landscape programs. The seven images shown here are from a series called Tall Ships.
