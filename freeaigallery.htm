We want your (AI) Artificial Intelligence art. Your AI art gallery here is free.

All art is permanently posted to our AI exhibit page at:
AI Exhibits

MOCA welcomes submission of your AI, Generative or Machine Art, regardless of the progam(s) used. Your submission and gallery are free. There are no membership or registration requirements and never any charge. Submissions are by email attachment.

JPG format only. Maximum size each image 300 kilobytes. Upload no less than five and no more than 12 AI images.

Note on image title
Image titles should be as brief and succinct as possible. The title should serve as the image name. For instance, if your title is Hanging Clothes Out to Dry, your image should be named "hangingclothesouttodry.jpg" (without the quotation marks).

TO SUBMIT, EMAIL YOUR AI ART BY EMAIL SUBMISSION TO:
don@moca.virtual.museum
or
donarcher@moca.virtual.museum

In the body of the email please feel free to add a biographical note and/or curriculum vitae and make a brief comment on your AI art and the manner of its construction.

Thank you for your submission.
